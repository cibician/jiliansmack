2am TV No Ones Watching

An animation made at Goldsmiths University about our (my) toxic relationship with screens. Do our devices love us as much as we love them? “Lovely human, you watch me every day and night till late. TVs aren't human, but we're clearly in love and I'll sing this again at 2.30 AM”
