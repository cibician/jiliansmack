Delicatoni

Delicatoni is an Italian music project that started in 2020 with the collaboration between Antonio Bettini, Giorgio Manzardo, Smilian Jack Cibic, and Claudio Murru. The diverse musical backgrounds and interests of the band crystallized into a dynamic mix of orchestral, electronic, jazz, Latin, and pop music.

Delicatoni has had the fortune to travel around Italy, touring and collaborating with emerging Italian and international artists — from painters and visual artists such as Leonardo Devito, Vittorio Dal Maso, Goodboyevil, Stefania Sbrighi, Bianca Girardi, Lucrezia Vicedomini, and Nicolò Bassetto, to musicians and producers such as Nice Elevator, Coca Puma, Coquinati, xmal, Lamante, and Tripolare.

Within Delicatoni, Ian is a composer, producer, synth nerd, graphic designer, and animator, always looking for visual elements to match the spirit of the music.
