About

Smilian Jack Cibic, a.k.a. Ian Cibic, is a music, drawing and people person. He spends his time between London and Northern Italy. After graduating in media & communications at Goldsmiths University, Ian has worked as a musician, animator, writer and events coordinator. Thanks to his friend Yev, he can also code a website.
