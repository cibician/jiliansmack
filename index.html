Delicatoni

Delicatoni is an Italian music project that started in 2020 with the collaboration between Antonio Bettini, Giorgio Manzardo, Smilian Jack Cibic, and Claudio Murru. The diverse musical backgrounds and interests of the band crystallized into a dynamic mix of orchestral, electronic, jazz, Latin, and pop music.

Delicatoni has had the fortune to travel around Italy, touring and collaborating with emerging Italian and international artists — from painters and visual artists such as Leonardo Devito, Vittorio Dal Maso, Goodboyevil, Stefania Sbrighi, Bianca Girardi, Lucrezia Vicedomini, and Nicolò Bassetto, to musicians and producers such as Nice Elevator, Coca Puma, Coquinati, xmal, Lamante, and Tripolare.

Within Delicatoni, Ian is a composer, producer and creative director always looking for visual elements to match the spirit of the music.

Milan Drawing Week

In 2023, I was asked to create the drawings for the identity of Milan Drawing Week, a city-wide festival dedicated to contemporary drawing, bringing together galleries, museums, and independent spaces across Milan to celebrate the language of drawing in all its forms.

about

Smilian Jack Cibic, a.k.a. Ian Cibic, is a music, drawing and people person. He spends his time between London and Northern Italy. After graduating in media & communications at Goldsmiths University, Ian has worked as a musician, animator, writer and events coordinator. Thanks to his friend Yev, he can also code a website.
